A peaceful enclave in the heart of the city, The Resident Liverpool offers a unique combination of comfort, design and heartfelt hospitality. As The Resident does not offer an in-house bar or restaurant, it’s ideally suited to the RopeWalks, Liverpool city centre’s vibrant design quarter. Enjoy exclusive discounts at some of Liverpool’s best bars and restaurants, with which this boutique hotel is surrounded and sample the best recommendations our thoughtful 'Resident Insider' teams have to offer.
